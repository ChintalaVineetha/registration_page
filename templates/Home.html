Monitoring the traffic on the homepage can help you determine the impact of your welcome message. Measure the number of times visitors fulfilled the call to action and visited the rest of the website. For instance, if the metrics showed high traffic on the inventory page, then you can conclude consumers felt inclined to look at your products, which can represent the appeal of your homepage. Consider implementing different welcome messages and using the analytics results to find the image or text that is most impactful.
